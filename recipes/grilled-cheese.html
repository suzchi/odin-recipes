Grilled Cheese

How to make the classic sandwich. Serves 2.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat pan over medium heat. Generously butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place 2 pieces of bread butter-side-down onto skillet bottom and add 1 slice of cheese. Place the remaining two slices of bread butter-side-up on top of the cheese.
  3. Grill until lightly browned then flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted.
